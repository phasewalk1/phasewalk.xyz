Verify the Copyright

We are not making any legally binding statements on the copyright status of the linked books/works. We strongly recommend that you verify the copyright status yourself. Possible approaches are

  • Consulting the frontmatter of the text.
  • Consulting the homepage if one has been given.
  • Using a search engine to find when, by whom and under which conditions the item has first been made available.
